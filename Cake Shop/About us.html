Our Story

Thirty-five years ago, a talented lady set out on a quest to provide the people of Sri Lanka with a pure taste experience. She has made mouth-watering delicious cakes that soon talked of town using only the most sumptuous ingredients and her own impeccable, good taste.


From fluffy, golden sponges covered in creamy butter icing to gooey, melt in the mouth chocolate concoctions, everything she touched was transformed into delectable mouthfuls of sheer delight.


Today, her simple desire to spread the good taste widely has resulted in an impressive array of charming cakes, desserts and other delicacies that continues to tangle with each bite.


So drop in and let us thrill your taste buds with some of the best tasting cakes in town.

Founded in 1985, Fanny's cake has been serving our proud customers for quite some time now, becoming the most popular Dessert Shop around. We are a proud member of the Colombo local merchant community, and love every opportunity to interact with our customers as we provide them with the freshest and most delicious ingredients. We have a wide selection of quality products that we’re sure you’ll just love. Stop by to have a taste..
